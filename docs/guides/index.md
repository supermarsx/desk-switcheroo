# Guides

Task-focused walkthroughs for getting the most out of Desk Switcheroo. Each guide takes
one area of the app — coloring and theming, desktop management, persistence and profiles,
the rules engine and hooks, or logging and diagnostics — and explains how it works, how
to configure it, and where the underlying behavior lives in the code.

Start anywhere; the pages cross-link to the [Configuration](../configuration/index.md)
section for the exact INI keys and to the [Reference](../reference/index.md) section for
deeper technical detail. The pages below are listed in the sidebar.
